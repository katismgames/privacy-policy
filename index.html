Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [2025-11-10]

Katism Games (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates mobile applications (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App. By downloading or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

This version includes: Firebase, AdMob, ironSource (Unity LevelPlay), Unity Ads, Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, and pseudonymized device info. Push notifications, GPS, contacts are not used.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum amount of data necessary to operate and improve the App.

1.1 Information You Provide

  • We do not require you to register an account or provide personal information to play the game.
  • If you contact us for support, we may collect your email address solely to respond to your inquiry.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, limited technical information may be collected automatically for analytics, debugging, and advertising purposes, including:

  • Operating system version (e.g., Android 14, iOS 17).
  • Device model and a pseudonymized device identifier derived from the device name using a one-way SHA-256 hash. The original device name is not stored or transmitted.
  • App version, language setting, gameplay events, session length, and crash logs.
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS / GAID on Android) for ad delivery and frequency capping.
  • Approximate location based on IP address (country-level only).

We do not collect: precise GPS location, contact lists, photos, camera, microphone, or push notification tokens in the current version of the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain the App.
  • To improve gameplay performance and stability.
  • To analyze usage trends and fix crashes.
  • To display and measure in-app advertisements.
  • To verify purchases and prevent fraud.
  • To comply with legal obligations if required.

Data is used in aggregate or pseudonymized form whenever possible.

When you choose to sign in, these services may provide your name and email address as authorized by you. We use this data solely for authentication and do not store or use it beyond that purpose.
