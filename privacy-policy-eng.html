Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-11-10

Katism Games (hereinafter referred to as the “Company”) operates a mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how the Company collects, uses, and protects personal information when users use the App.

This version includes Firebase, AdMob, ironSource (Unity LevelPlay), Unity Ads, Google Login, Apple Login, and hashed device information. Push notifications, GPS, and contact information are not collected.

1. Personal Information Collected

1.1 Information Provided Directly by Users

  • The App does not require separate registration or submission of personal information to use the game.
  • When submitting customer inquiries, minimal contact information such as an email address may be collected.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Operating system (OS) version (e.g., Android 14, iOS 17)
  • A pseudonymized identifier generated by applying a SHA-256 one-way hash to the device model name and device name (the original information is neither stored nor transmitted)
  • App version, language settings, session duration, crash logs, and gameplay events
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID) – used for ad delivery and frequency capping
  • Country code (e.g., KR, US) and language settings – used for service usage statistics and service operation improvement
  • During service provision, certain third-party SDKs (such as Firebase and advertising SDKs) may temporarily process communication identifiers (IP address) to generate statistical information (e.g., country or regional-level aggregated data). The Company does not store such IP addresses or use them for location-based services.

The Company does not collect the following information: GPS location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, push tokens.

2. Purpose of Use of Personal Information

  • Operation and maintenance of the App
  • Improvement of game performance and stability
  • Usage analysis and error correction
  • Provision of in-app advertisements and measurement of advertising effectiveness
  • Verification of purchase history and prevention of fraudulent use
  • Compliance with legal obligations

Collected information is used only in statistical or pseudonymized form whenever possible.

3. Authentication Services (Google / Apple Login)

The App may use the following authentication services for account linking and data storage:

  • Google Login (Google LLC)
  • Apple Login (Apple Inc.)

Within the scope of user consent, basic profile information such as name and email address may be provided. The Company uses such information solely for authentication purposes and does not store it separately.

5. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through Google Play or the Apple App Store. The Company does not collect or store payment card information. Only minimal non-identifiable information such as purchase tokens, product IDs, and purchase timestamps is received.

6. Provision and Disclosure of Personal Information

  • The Company does not sell or rent users’ personal information.
  • Data may be provided to third-party service providers to the extent necessary for service provision.
  • Information may be disclosed only when required by law.

7. Children’s Privacy

This App is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If the Company becomes aware that such information has been collected, it will be deleted immediately.

9. Retention Period and Security Measures

The Company deletes collected information without delay once the purpose of collection has been achieved. Hashed identifiers and analytics data are securely stored in a form that cannot directly identify individuals.

10. Cross-Border Data Transfers

The Company may process personal information on servers located in the Republic of Korea, the United States, or other countries for service operation. By using the App, users are deemed to have consented to such transfers.
